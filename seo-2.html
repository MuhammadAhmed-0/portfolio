How I Increased Organic Traffic by 90% for a SaaS AI Website Building Tool

The Story

I had the opportunity to work with Website Building AI Tool, a cutting-edge B2B SaaS company specializing in AI-powered website building solutions. As they aimed to dominate the competitive market, my mission was clear: to drive substantial organic traffic to their platform and establish them as industry leaders.

Client Challenge

The Client faced numerous challenges typical of emerging B2B SaaS companies. Despite having an innovative product, their online presence was limited, with stagnant organic traffic. They needed to differentiate themselves from competitors, improve search engine rankings, and attract high-quality leads to their site. The challenge was not just to increase traffic but to ensure that the traffic was highly relevant, leading to conversions.

My Strategy

To address these challenges, I implemented a strategic SEO plan:

  1. Website Audit:

    First, I conducted a comprehensive audit to identify technical issues, optimize site speed, and ensure mobile responsiveness.

  2. Keyword Research:

    After that, I performed in-depth keyword research to uncover high-impact terms that competitors had missed, focusing on those most relevant to the AI website building tools niche.

  3. On-Page Optimization:

    Then, I optimized website elements, including titles, descriptions, headings, and internal linking, to enhance search engine crawlability and user experience.

  4. Content Optimization:

    Now, I revamped existing content and created new, high-quality content targeting the identified keywords, ensuring it resonated with the target audience and aligned with search engine algorithms.

  5. Off-Page SEO:

    Finally, I executed a robust off-page SEO strategy, including quality backlink acquisition through guest post, Niche edits and other methods, to further boost search engine rankings and domain authority.

The Resuls

The client saw a significant increase in organic traffic to their website after implementing SEO strategy. Here are some of the results:

  • 90% increase in organic traffic
  • 75% increase in organic Session
  • 60% increase in organic traffic Value
  • 140% increase in keywords ranking in top 10 of SERP
