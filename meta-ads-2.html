How I Achieved 5.02 ROAS for a SaaS Company through Facebook Ads

The Story

I worked with a SaaS company that specialized in business automation tools. They had been running Facebook Ads for customer acquisition, but their campaigns were underperforming, leading to high costs and a poor return on ad spend (ROAS). The client approached me to help improve their ad performance and scale their marketing efforts while maintaining profitability.

Client Challenge

The client's Facebook Ads account was underperforming, with three key challenges: a low return on ad spend (ROAS), a high cost per acquisition (CPA), and an inability to scale campaigns effectively. Despite their efforts, the ads were not generating a sufficient return on investment, and the cost of acquiring new customers was higher than desired. Additionally, when they tried to increase their ad spend to reach more users, their campaign performance suffered, making it difficult to achieve their marketing goals.

My Strategy

To overcome these challenges, I implemented a data-driven Facebook Ads strategy focused on maximizing ROAS while keeping costs under control:

  1. Existing Campaign Audit
  2. Refined Compaign Strategy
  3. Develop High-Performing Lookalike Audiences
  4. Create Custom Audiences from Existing Data
  5. Optimize Ad Creatives for Key Benefits and Features
  6. Use A/B Testing for Best-Performing Creatives
  7. Set Up Retargeting Campaigns for Engagement
  8. Run Upsell Campaigns for Existing Customers
  9. Optimize Budgets and Bids for Best Performance
  10. Monitor and Reporting to the Client

The Results

The campaign performance was outstanding, achieving remarkable results across all key metrics:




  • ROAS Increased to 5.02
  • 19% Decrease Cost Per Purchase
  • Consistent Sales Growth
